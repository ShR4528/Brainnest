Wayne Gretzky

Wayne Douglas Gretzky is a Canadian former professional ice hockey player and former head coach. He played 20 seasons in the National Hockey League (NHL) for four teams from 1979 to 1999. Nicknamed "The Great", many have called him the greatest hockey player of all time. sportswriters, players, and the NHL itself. Gretzky is the leading scorer in NHL history, with more goals and assists than any other player.

Sidney Crosby

Sidney Patrick Crosby is a Canadian ice hockey player, captain and center forward of the Pittsburgh Penguins club, who selected the Canadian under the overall first number in the NHL Entry Draft 2005. Two-time Olympic champion (2010, 2014), world champion (2015), three-time Stanley Cup winner (2009, 2016, 2017), member of the Triple Golden Club.
